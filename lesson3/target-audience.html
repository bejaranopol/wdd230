Target Audience

My audience is all people interested in dining out, entertainment, families, free time for all ages. The site will be used approximately by people between the ages of 13 to 60+. I chose this population because in my city people are looking for rustic places/restaurants.

Scenarios

  • 1) Looking to know the lunch/dinner menu
  • 2) Looking for the touristy or rustic atmosphere.
  • 3) Looking for the place that is closest.
  • 4) Looking for promotions/offers and the quality of the service (opinions).
  • 5) Looking for what is the history of the restaurant.
